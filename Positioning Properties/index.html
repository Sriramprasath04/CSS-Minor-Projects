    Absolute

  • Losses its positional space and changes it's position according to its parent position
  • In case-1 (Absolute-1), it's parent is body
  • In case-2 (Absolute-2), it's parent is a div ( container has to hold the property of relative )

    Relative

  • Holds its positional space and changes it's position according to its origin
  • In case-1 (Relative-1), it has moven to left 100px
  • In case-2 (Relative-2), it has moven to 20px top, 100px left from its origin

    • Fixed

  • Losses its positional space and changes it's position according to its body not regording to its parent position
  • In case-1 (Fixed-1), it has moven to 500px top and 30px right regardless to its origin
  • In case-2 (Fixed-2), it has moven to 20px top, 110px right regardless to its origin
